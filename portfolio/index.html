About Me

While living in Australia, I decided I wanted a change of direction. I had spent most of my working life in construction and engineering with some customer service jobs along the way.


Initially, I began a Computing and Engineering certificate with the Open University. I chose this one as I was unsure what path I wanted to go down. I’m fascinated by technology and with my previous experience in engineering, furthering my education there just made sense. But it wasn’t until I started progressing through my modules that I realized I wanted to dedicate all my time to the computing side, so I dropped my engineering modules.


The course has given me great insight into the industry, but since beginning, I have found more interest in programming and have been working on a full-stack bootcamp with Codecademy along with the computing certificate.
